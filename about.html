I originally made these masks for a Dungeons and Dragons game, but they're pretty good for filling up space on the web page. Each mask is a different generation of the same role. Kinda like Black Panther or Avatar the Last Airbender.
----------------------------------
Stiki co. prides itself for it's sustainably sourced, high quality stickers.

Our stickers are produced by a small Himalayan village who have honed the practiced of sticker making for hundreds of generations.
Each sticker is woven from the fibers of the ancient Gasto Gasto tree, the last of its kind.

Our sticker glue is a secret mixture of elements found only at the edges of the world, carried thousands of miles by llama. The glue it produces is non-acidic, non-toxic, and somehow only sticks to the things you want it to.

The artwork for our stickers are each individually oil painted by masters of their craft.

The posters are printed at the kinkos down the street.
