MEET THE TIKI

The OG Masked Warrior - Oct 14, 2018

Once a generation or so, a child is born in a Uthgardt tribe with rock skin and mystic powers. They are known only as "Tikis" and serve as protectors of the tribes, and keeper of the forests.

Being the first of his kind, he was simply known as "The Tiki". He was said to be unpredictable, and was feared by his enemies and allies alike.

MEET OCHRE

The Wandering Sorceror - Oct 1, 2018

Myconids or "Mushroom Men" found Ochre as an infant, abandoned by his parents. They took him in because they believed he could be a communicator between races in.

The Myconids raised him like on of them, and Ochre used his ability to speak to help the Myconids trade with the dark elves and the deep gnomes and parlay for safe passage.

Gnomes helped his transition to the outside world. The deep gnomes taught him common and how to hide, the forest gnomes showed him how to survive in the wilderness and helped him hone his magical abilities, and rock gnomes taught him basic rules and customs to live with other intelligent creatures.
